### 2. Content Strategy

**Output Example**:
```
LinkedIn Content Strategy: B2B SaaS Founder

CONTENT GOALS
─────────────
- Build thought leadership in AI/SaaS space
- Generate inbound leads for consulting/advising
- Attract talent for hiring
- Support company brand awareness

TARGET AUDIENCE
───────────────
Primary: Startup founders, tech executives
Secondary: VCs, potential employees, media
Interests: AI, startups, productivity, leadership

CONTENT PILLARS (5)
───────────────────

1. Founder Journey (30%)
   - Wins and lessons
   - Behind-the-scenes
   - Vulnerable moments
   - Milestones and celebrations

2. Industry Insights (25%)
   - AI trends and analysis
   - Market observations
   - Predictions
   - Hot takes

3. Tactical How-Tos (20%)
   - Step-by-step guides
   - Templates and frameworks
   - Tool recommendations
   - Process breakdowns

4. Team & Culture (15%)
   - Hiring stories
   - Team wins
   - Culture insights
   - Remote work tips

5. Engagement/Personal (10%)
   - Questions and polls
   - Personal stories
   - Industry events
   - Celebrations

POSTING SCHEDULE
────────────────

Frequency: 5x per week (weekdays)
Best times: 8-10 AM or 5-6 PM (your audience's timezone)

Weekly Calendar:
- Monday: Industry insight/hot take
- Tuesday: Tactical how-to
- Wednesday: Founder journey story
- Thursday: Team/culture post
- Friday: Engagement post (question/poll)

POST FORMATS
────────────

Format 1: Story Post (Highest engagement)
─────────────────────────────────────────
Structure:
- Hook (first line)
- Story/context
- Lesson/insight
- Takeaway
- CTA

Example:
"I almost shut down my company last year.

We had 3 months of runway. No new customers in 
weeks. My co-founder wanted to quit.

Then I made one change that saved everything:

[Story continues...]

The lesson? [Insight]

Have you ever been close to giving up? 
What kept you going?"

Format 2: List Post (High saves)
────────────────────────────────
Structure:
- Hook with number
- Numbered list
- Brief explanation each
- CTA

Example:
"7 AI tools I use daily (and you should too):

1. [Tool] - for [use case]
2. [Tool] - for [use case]
...

Which ones are you using? Any I should add?"

Format 3: Carousel/Document (High reach)
────────────────────────────────────────
Structure:
- Cover slide (hook)
- 8-12 content slides
- CTA slide

Topics that work:
- Step-by-step guides
- Frameworks
- Comparisons
- Lessons learned

Format 4: Poll (High engagement)
────────────────────────────────
Structure:
- Context/question
- 4 options
- Follow-up in comments

Example:
"What's your biggest challenge with AI tools?

🔵 Too many options
🟢 Hard to learn
🟡 Expensive
🔴 Don't see ROI

I'll share resources based on the top answer!"

Format 5: Hot Take (High comments)
──────────────────────────────────
Structure:
- Controversial statement
- Your reasoning
- Invitation to disagree

Example:
"Unpopular opinion: Most AI startups will fail 
in the next 2 years.

Here's why:
[Reasoning]

Change my mind 👇"

HOOK FORMULAS
─────────────

Story hooks:
- "I made a $100k mistake last month."
- "My biggest regret as a founder:"
- "3 years ago, I was [situation]. Today, [result]."

Curiosity hooks:
- "Nobody talks about this, but..."
- "The real reason [thing] happens:"
- "I've never shared this publicly before."

Value hooks:
- "Here's exactly how I [achieved result]:"
- "The framework that changed everything:"
- "Stop doing [thing]. Do this instead:"

Controversial hooks:
- "Unpopular opinion:"
- "[Common belief] is wrong. Here's why:"
- "I disagree with [famous person] on this:"

ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY
───────────────────

Before posting:
- Engage for 15-30 min on others' posts
- Comment thoughtfully (not just "Great post!")
- Build relationships with key accounts

After posting:
- Respond to every comment in first 2 hours
- Ask follow-up questions
- Thank people for sharing

Daily habits:
- 30 min engagement minimum
- Comment on 10-15 posts
- Send 5 connection requests with notes
- Check and respond to DMs

METRICS TO TRACK
────────────────

Weekly:
- Impressions
- Engagement rate
- Follower growth
- Profile views
- Connection requests

Monthly:
- Top performing posts
- Content pillar performance
- Inbound leads/opportunities
- Speaking/podcast invitations

Benchmarks:
- Engagement rate: 2-5% (good), 5%+ (excellent)
- Follower growth: 5-10% monthly
- Profile views: 100+ weekly
```

## Best Practices

### Do ✅
- Post consistently (algorithm rewards it)
- Engage before and after posting
- Use native content (not external links)
- Tell stories, not just share info
- Be authentic and vulnerable
- Respond to every comment
- Build relationships, not just followers
- Track what works and iterate

### Don't ❌
- Post and ghost
- Be overly promotional
- Use too many hashtags (3-5 max)
- Share only company content
- Ignore comments and DMs
- Buy followers or engagement
- Post the same content repeatedly
- Be inauthentic or corporate
