## Best Practices

### Do ✅
- Test everything (A/B test)
- Focus on customer problems, not features
- Use social proof extensively
- Create urgency authentically
- Segment your audience
- Track attribution carefully
- Iterate based on data
- Build relationships, not just leads

### Don't ❌
- Spam your email list
- Make false claims
- Ignore mobile experience
- Skip the follow-up
- Neglect existing customers
- Over-complicate funnels
- Forget about retention
- Copy competitors blindly
