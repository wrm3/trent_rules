Codex is rapidly becoming one of the most powerful ways to build,
driven by builders who share real-world workflows and lessons with
each other.

Codex Ambassadors are community organizers, open-source maintainers,
student leaders, and power users who actively spread what works, make
Codex easier to adopt in practice, and help shape where it goes next.

[Apply Today](https://openai.com/form/codex-ambassadors)

[Upcoming Meetups](https://developers.openai.com/codex/community/meetups)

![Codex Ambassadors leading a community workshop](https://developers.openai.com/images/codex/ambassadors/ambassadors-18.jpg)![Builders collaborating during a Codex Ambassador event](https://developers.openai.com/images/codex/ambassadors/ambassadors-25.jpg)

Ambassadors run hands-on meetups, workshops, and community sessions
around the world.

## What you’ll do

As a Codex Ambassador, you’ll join a small global cohort and partner
with OpenAI to:

- Run hands-on Codex events in your local community
- Create reusable learning assets others can build on
- Experiment with ideas to grow and support builder communities

- Share candid, real-world feedback directly with the Codex team


## Who should apply

We’re looking for people with hands-on experience leading or
supporting developer communities, like running meetups, maintaining
open-source projects, teaching workshops, or regularly helping
others learn how to build.

## Support from OpenAI

- Codex credits to support your own work and power local events

- Ready-to-use starter kits you can tailor to your community
- A direct line to fellow Ambassadors and the Codex team for
collaboration and feedback

- Invitations to future exclusive events where you can meet the
Codex team

- Exclusive swag and a honorarium for your time and contributions


This is a two-way program, and will also evolve our support based on
what the cohort learns on the ground.

**Time commitment:** ~2–4 hours per week

## Bring your community with you

If you like bringing people together to build, learn, and share,
and you're excited to help shape what a great ambassador program
can be, we'd love to hear from you.

[Start your application](https://openai.com/form/codex-ambassadors)
